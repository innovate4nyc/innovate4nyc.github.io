Mayor's Office of Operations: Innovation and Design Team

Put your skills into service. Improve the lives of millions of New Yorkers.

We are the go to resource for harnessing digital tools and capabilities to improve the lives of low-income and vulnerable New Yorkers. As part of the New York City Mayor’s Office of Operations, the Accelerator + Connect unit uses policy, management and technical expertise to help produce better outcomes for residents.

As part of this unit, the Innovation and Design Team works with policy teams, City agencies, community-based organizations, and end users to analyze needs and opportunities and turn them into actionable digital services than can be designed, prototyped, tested, and developed.


  Our Work

The unit currently operates HHS Accelerator, Worker Connect, and ACCESS NYC. Together, these initiatives break information silos through the use of modernized technology and coordinated agency practices to more efficiently and effectively provide health and human services to New Yorkers. We are actively working on new initiatives that build upon existing products and our expertise areas, including reducing the burden on non-profits, facilitating data integration to improve social service performance, and easing access to services and programs.

The Innovation and Design Team is central to the work on all products and new initiatives by leading design and user experience, producing design materials, conducting user research, working closely with the internal and vendor technology teams for development, and setting ongoing strategy for products.


  Join the Team

An apprenticeship with our team is an opportunity to grow your expertise in design, digital services or public policy and help improve the lives of millions of New Yorkers. We are currently seeking apprentices with an interest in design, product management, data visualization or public policy.

Apprenticeships are project-based, part-time and usually last 2-3 months. Projects will focus on the delivery, on-going success, and continuous improvement of digital services.

Skills We’re Seeking

Commitment to the mission of advancing greater equality and opportunity.

Design in any/all of the following practices: user experience, human centered design, interaction design, user research, data visualization, and service design.

Excitement about practicing human-centered design in government.

Background or interest in design, product development, public policy, health or social impact.

Turning user research insights into actionable insights and materials, such as user experience flows.

Excellent communication and presentation skills, both verbal and written.
